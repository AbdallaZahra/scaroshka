Exacutiv summary

In this proposal, you’ll come to see us as visual art makers, creativity seekers, and unconventional storytellers—all integral aspects of our mission. Together, these identities paint a full picture: we are creators on a mission to redefine visual artistry through boundary-pushing storytelling, fueled by a unwavering pursuit of creativity in every design and story. Our startup isn’t simply about producing unique clothing; it’s about creating wearable art pieces with collectible value. Each collection is part of a larger, evolving narrative world that expresses our identity through surreal, curiosity-provoking stories designed to captivate our audience. This world is brought to life through fictional characters who embody universal human emotions—fear, joy, anger, curiosity, and more These stories will be told through 3D animations and translated into captivating designs that appear as prints on our apparel.
