Mission and vision

We aim to transform designs and animations into wearable expressions of identity and storytelling. Our mission is to share our stories in a way that inspires and immerses, creating a fictional world that reveals the wonders and mysteries of life and reflects these insights in every design. Our vision is to redefine how people experience clothing, turning each piece into a portal to an imaginative world. We aspire to extend our creative concepts beyond fashion into other lifestyle products, inspiring others to envision and create their own worlds. Ultimately, we aim to develop a cartoon series featuring our original characters in evolving, engaging plots.

core values

Creativity : We push the boundaries of fashion and design, constantly exploring new approaches to tell our stories in fresh and imaginative ways.
Authenticity : We believe in expressing our unique vision with integrity and originality, ensuring our designs stay true to our brand identity.
Simplicity : We present our ideas with clarity and simplicity while embracing innovation, especially in 3D animation and digital art.
Independence : We value our creative independence, a principle evident in our commitment to building a distinctive fictional universe.
